Why this matters

We built scripts that count words to answer questions about women in General Conference. But words are not concepts. The word "mother" appears in "Heavenly Mother" (theology), "my mother" (personal story), and "mother earth" (metaphor). If we count all of those as "motherhood," our conclusions are wrong.
