Metric modes explained:
Per 10k words — frequency normalized by total words spoken. "How dense is this word in speech?" Fair comparison across groups of different sizes.
Raw count — just how many times the word appears. Bigger groups say everything more, so this is best for seeing absolute volume.
% of talks — what percentage of talks contain this word at least once. "How widespread is this topic?" A word could appear 50 times in 1 talk or 1 time in 50 talks — this tells you which.
Per talk avg — average number of times the word appears per talk. "When someone DOES talk, how much do they say it?"
